1. What is BBVP?

BBVP is a disciplined engineering method that forces every feature to go through four steps before it is considered real:

  • Build — Create the feature cleanly in isolation.
  • Back — Produce documentation, evidence packs, logs and safety notes.
  • Verify — Test ON/OFF states, boundaries, failures, and cross-region behaviour.
  • Prove — Provide traceable proofs (SHA-256 logs, WORM entries, test output).

Why Saami created this system

In the digital health world, shortcuts create long-term risks. Saami (Sami) Salami designed BBVP because families, ministries, and courts deserve to see that the platform is built on real evidence — not marketing.

2. No “Done” until 100% verified

Under BBVP, a feature is NEVER considered “done” unless:

  • Feature flag exists
  • Feature flag OFF = safe behaviour (usually 503)
  • Feature flag ON = full functionality
  • Both states tested and logged
  • Evidence pack generated

This method protects real families and ensures integrity even under legal or regulatory scrutiny.
